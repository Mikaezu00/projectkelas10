COFFEE-BASED DRINK

Selamat Datang di Kopi Senja, tempat dimana kelezatan dan kehangatan menyatu dengan senja.

Selamatkan momen indahmu dengan secangkir kopi sempurna di bawah cahaya senja yang memikat.

Terdapat banyak varian kopi untuk semua kalangan mulai dari cappuccino, americano, mocha dan latte.

Latte

a combination of strong espresso and perfectly prepared milk

Cappucino

consists of three layers made of espresso, hot milk, and milk foam

Mocha

mixing espresso, milk, chocolate, and often added whipped cream on top

Americano

made by extracting ground coffee with hot water under high pressure

Matcha

Milk

Juice
